Chute, Marchette. “Drinking Fountain.” Read-Aloud Rhymes for the Very Young. Selected by Jack Prelutsky.
Illustrated by Marc Brown. New York: Knopf, 1986. (1957)
When I climb up
To get a drink,
It doesn’t work
The way you’d think.
I turn it up,
The water goes
And hits me right
Upon the nose.
I turn it down
To make it small
And don’t get any
Drink at all.
