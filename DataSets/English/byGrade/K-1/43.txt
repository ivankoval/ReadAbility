“Po Po,” Shang shouted, but there was no answer.
“Po Po,” Tao shouted, but there was no answer.
“Po Po,” Paotze shouted. There was still no answer. The children climbed to the branches just above the wolf and saw
that he was truly dead. Then they climbed down, went into the house, closed the door, locked the door with the latch
and fell peacefully asleep.
On the next day their mother returned with baskets of food from their real Po Po, and the three sisters told her the
story of the Po Po who had come.
